What We Do

Comprehensive Commercial Services

From sourcing a qualified supplier to advising a board on market entry — our services cover the full commercial lifecycle.

01

Local Network & Representation

We act as your in-market representative across GCC and MENA, giving international manufacturers and suppliers a credible, senior presence without the cost of a local office. We navigate vendor registration, regulatory requirements and key stakeholder relationships on your behalf.

02

Sourcing & Supply Chain

We identify, qualify and manage reliable suppliers for industrial materials and equipment across the Oil & Gas, Power and Construction sectors. Our supply chain expertise ensures competitive pricing, regulatory compliance and on-time delivery for your critical procurement needs.

03

Tendering & Bid Management

We manage the full tender lifecycle — from opportunity identification and pre-qualification to technical submissions and commercial negotiation. We prepare compliant, competitive bids for NOC, EPC and government tenders across the region, maximising your win probability.

04

Market Intelligence

We deliver actionable intelligence on market dynamics, competitor positioning, project pipelines and procurement trends across GCC and MENA. Our reports are designed for decision-makers — not generic research, but targeted analysis that informs your commercial strategy.

05

Procurement Management

We optimise your procurement process end-to-end — vendor qualification, RFQ preparation, commercial evaluation, contract negotiation and order tracking. Particularly valuable for international companies managing Gulf-based projects remotely or entering regional procurement for the first time.

06

Board & Executive Advisory

We advise international boards and senior management teams on Gulf market entry strategies, risk assessment, partner selection and commercial positioning. Our counsel is built on ground-level market experience — not theoretical frameworks.
